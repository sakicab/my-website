Lighten your day with some short and funny true stories.

The Elevator Button

I once pressed the elevator button repeatedly thinking it would make it come faster. A little kid next to me said, “You know it won’t come quicker, right?” I nodded. Then he added, “Adults are weird.” I agreed.

The Email Mistake

I once sent an email complaining about my boss... to my boss. He replied, "Agreed." We never talked about it again.

Misheard Lyrics

For years, I thought the lyrics were “Sweet dreams are made of cheese.” I only found out the truth last year. Honestly, I still prefer my version.
